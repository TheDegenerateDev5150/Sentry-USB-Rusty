# Sentry Cloud

**Your drives, in the cloud — only you can read them.**

Sentry Cloud is the companion service to Sentry USB. Drives recorded on your Pi upload automatically and show up on any device you're signed in on.

## How it works

- Each route is **encrypted on the Pi before it leaves your network**. The cloud stores the ciphertext and can't read it.
- Drives sync when your Pi is on WiFi.
- View your drives from any device by signing in to **[sentryusb.com](https://sentryusb.com)**.
